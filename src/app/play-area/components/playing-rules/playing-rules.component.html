Objective

The objective of Sudoku is to fill a 9x9 grid with digits so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3x3 subgrids contains all of the digits from 1 to 9.

Basic Rules
  • Fill the Grid: Complete the 9x9 grid with digits from 1 to 9.
  • Unique Digits: Each digit must appear exactly once in each row, column, and 3x3 subgrid.
  • No Repeats: Avoid repeating the same digit in any row, column, or 3x3 subgrid.
Hints and Tips
  • Start with Easy Numbers: Look for cells with few possibilities and start there.
  • Use Pencil Marks: Write possible numbers in empty cells to keep track of options.
  • Look for Patterns: Identify patterns and constraints in the grid to solve the puzzle more efficiently.
Common Mistakes to Avoid
  • Ignoring Constraints: Ensure all constraints are met before finalizing numbers.
  • Overlooking Hidden Possibilities: Check all possible numbers for each cell before making a move.
  • Making Assumptions: Solve the puzzle logically and avoid guessing as much as possible.

Enjoy playing Sudoku and have fun sharpening your logical skills!
